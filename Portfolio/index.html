About Me

My name is Wiktoria. I have been working as a graphic designer for three years. I mainly designed outdoor and print advertising (Behance) . In early 2020 I decided to expand my abilities and start learning front-end programming languages.

At first I studied myself an focused on the theoretical part, next I took 3-month course front-end Developer by Akademia 108, where I started to practice basic knowlege.

Now I am currently focused on continuously improving my skills by doing small projects using ifferent technologies.
